About

The Designer
Behind the Click

I am Varun — a YouTube thumbnail designer who turns scroll-past moments into click-through conversions. I craft high-CTR thumbnails built around visual psychology, bold typography, and colour theory.

My process starts with understanding your video's core promise — then designing a thumbnail that communicates it in under two seconds of glance time. Every element earns its place on the canvas.

No stock-template thinking. No cluttered compositions. Just clean, intentional visuals that work with the YouTube algorithm and your audience's psychology.

What I Do

I design custom YouTube thumbnails — from concept to final file — engineered to maximise click-through rate across gaming, tech, finance, lifestyle, and educational channels.

How I Do It

Visual hierarchy first, always. I study what performs in your niche, identify the visual gap, and design a thumbnail that stands out in a crowded feed while staying native to your brand.
